THE META

PHYSICAL POWER
Anarchy usually means no rules. Here, you are the rules. We turned server permissions into physical items. Power is something you can find, steal, and lose.

THE 7 GEMS
7 Ancient Gems exist in the world, each granting specific administrative powers. They are scarce. They are the only law. And they are scattered in the wilderness.

ETERNAL REVOLUTION
Rulers are not gods. Once a Gem is used, the power cycles. The Gem respawns randomly in the world. Stability is impossible. Revolution is inevitable.

FEATURES

  • THE 7 CROWNS - Find the Gems. Become the Admin. Lose it all.
  • EMPIRE BUILDING - Claim your land. Build your fortress. Defend your legacy.
  • THE METRO - A player-built underground highway connecting the chaos.
  • RAW SURVIVAL - No TP. No Home. No Mercy. Just you and the world.
