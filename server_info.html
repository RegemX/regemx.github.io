The Logic of Power

Order from Chaos

We stand on the shoulders of giants like 2b2t. Anarchy servers offer ultimate freedom, but often devolve into a static state of chaos where equality means no one truly possesses the power to build lasting order. It is chaos without direction.

We believe in a living, breathing hierarchy. A world where a ruling class exists to establish order, but that ruling class is never permanent. Power must be earned, defended, and inevitably, lost.

The RuleGems System

This is where RuleGems comes in. Seven ancient artifacts are scattered across the world. They are not just items; they are keys to the server itself.

Redemption - Finding and redeeming a Gem grants you administrative power. You become one of the Rulers (Server Admins).
Circulation - Once redeemed, the Gem does not stay with you. It vanishes and respawns randomly in the world, waiting for the next ambitious player.
Revolution - Your power is not safe. Any player who finds a respawned Gem can redeem it to challenge the status quo, potentially overturning existing Rulers.
